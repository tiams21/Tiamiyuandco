What we offer

Services built for
Nigerian businesses

From your first audit to complex tax structuring, we deliver professional financial services that help you stay compliant, grow confidently, and make better decisions.

01
Audit & Assurance
Statutory audits, internal control reviews, and assurance engagements that give stakeholders confidence in your financial statements.
02
Tax Consulting
Personal and corporate income tax, VAT, WHT, and full FIRS compliance. We handle your filings and represent you in tax matters.
03
Bookkeeping & Accounts
Accurate monthly bookkeeping, management accounts, and financial reporting for businesses that need clean numbers without the overhead.
04
Business Advisory
Financial planning, budgeting, cash flow management, and strategic advisory for SMEs navigating growth or restructuring.
05
Company Secretarial
CAC incorporation, annual returns, share restructuring, and all statutory compliance filings to keep your company in good standing.
06
Payroll Services
End-to-end payroll processing, PAYE remittance, and pension compliance so your people are paid correctly and on time.
